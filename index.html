How it works

More than flashcards

Luko combines active recall with motor memory — the way characters were always meant to be learned.

✍️

Stroke-by-stroke drawing

Practice writing each character with animated stroke guides and real-time feedback on your strokes.

🧠

Spaced repetition

Smart scheduling surfaces the right characters at the right time — so you study less and retain more.

📖

HSK vocabulary

Structured around official HSK levels, with pinyin, definitions, and example sentences for every character.

Native audio coming soon

Hear every character and word pronounced clearly, with tone markers to build accurate listening recognition.

Progress tracking

Track your daily streak, session scores, and mastery level for every character you've studied.
